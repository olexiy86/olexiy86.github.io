About me

Continuous learner who deeply passionate about data analysis and its applications into practice. With a keen interest in leveraging data-driven insights to optimize decision-making processes, I constantly explore new methodologies and tools to drive efficiency and innovation. Blending excellent work ethic, time management and dedication, I strive to achieve set goals. As a team player I communicate effectively and complement work of others when needed.

I have an extensive knowledge and expertise in Olympic and professional sports (especially sport climbing). My coaching background entails leading, mentoring, planning, organizing, researching, analyzing and managing projects. Along with coaching career I was lecturing (2008-2016) at the Dnipropetrovsk State Institute of Physical Education and Sports and in 2012 earned Ph.D. in Sport Science. I studied sport statistics and applied biomechanics.
